# 🍕 Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Pizza Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive data visualization project designed to analyze sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends within a pizza business.

This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights, enabling data-driven decision-making across sales, operations, and marketing functions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance and revenue trends
* Identify peak sales days and hours
* Evaluate product category and size performance
* Highlight top-performing and underperforming items
* Understand customer ordering behavior

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* 💰 **Total Revenue:** $817,860
* 🧾 **Total Orders:** 21,350
* 🍕 **Total Pizzas Sold:** 49,574
* 📦 **Average Order Value:** $38.31
* 🔢 **Average Pizzas per Order:** 2.32

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### ⏰ Sales Trends

* **Peak Days:** Friday & Saturday
* **Peak Hours:** 12 PM – 1 PM and 4 PM – 8 PM
* Clear upward trend in orders towards weekends

### 🍕 Category Insights

* **Classic Category** contributes the highest sales and order volume
* Balanced contribution from Supreme, Veggie, and Chicken categories

### 📏 Size Analysis

* **Large Size Pizzas** dominate sales contribution
* Medium size follows as the second most preferred choice

### 🏆 Best Sellers

* The Classic Deluxe Pizza
* Barbecue Chicken Pizza
* Hawaiian Pizza
* Pepperoni Pizza

### 📉 Worst Sellers

* Brie Carre Pizza (lowest performance)
* Mediterranean & Calabrese pizzas also underperform

## 💡 Key Insights

* Weekend evenings drive the highest revenue
* A limited set of products contributes significantly to total sales (Pareto effect)
* Large-size pizzas are the most profitable segment
* Some low-performing items can be optimized or removed from the menu

---

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the Power BI (.pbix) file
2. Use the **date slicer** to filter monthly trends
3. Explore:

   * Daily & hourly sales patterns
   * Category & size distribution
   * Top & bottom performing products
